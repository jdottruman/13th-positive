Our Mission

At 13th Positive, we are committed to building a stronger community by instilling hope, support, and positive values in children and their families. Through dedicated acts of service, mentorship, and goodwill, we aim to empower individuals to rise above challenges and embrace a future filled with opportunities.

Our purpose is to create lasting change in the lives of those we touch, nurturing a cycle of positivity and growth that echoes throughout Maywood and beyond.

Meet The Members

Our dedicated members of 13th Positive are the driving force behind our mission. Each individual brings unique skills, experiences, and passion to help us make a positive impact on our community. From our founders to our newest team members, their collective efforts and vision push us towards a brighter, more inclusive future.

How Contributions Are Used

Your support makes a meaningful impact! Every dollar we receive goes toward creating positive change within our community. Below is a breakdown of how your contributions will be used:

Community Programs

We develop and sustain initiatives focused on mental health, education, and youth empowerment to uplift the individuals we serve.

Events & Workshops

We organize events, workshops, and training sessions to promote growth, collaboration, and mental well-being.

Operational Costs

From maintaining our website to covering essential resources and tools, we ensure the organization runs efficiently.

Emergency Relief

A portion of donations is reserved for individuals in need of urgent support due to unexpected crises.

We are committed to transparency and ensuring that your contributions create lasting positive change. Thank you for your generosity and belief in our mission!

Local Resources

Local resources are vital for the success of the 13th Positive Foundation because they foster a strong sense of community and allow for direct, impactful engagement. By utilizing local businesses, organizations, and volunteers, the foundation can tailor its programs to meet the unique needs of the community it serves. Local resources also enhance trust and collaboration, as they are familiar with the challenges and opportunities within the area. Furthermore, partnering with local entities ensures sustainability, as it supports the local economy and encourages long-term involvement in the foundation’s initiatives.
