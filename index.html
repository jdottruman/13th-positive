Our Mission

At 13th Positive, we are committed to building a stronger community by instilling hope, support, and positive values in children and their families. Through dedicated acts of service, mentorship, and goodwill, we aim to empower individuals to rise above challenges and embrace a future filled with opportunities.

Our purpose is to create lasting change in the lives of those we touch, nurturing a cycle of positivity and growth that echoes throughout Maywood and beyond.

Members of 13th Postive

Our dedicated members of 13th Positive are the driving force behind our mission. Each individual brings unique skills, experiences, and passion to help us make a positive impact on our community. From our founders to our newest team members, their collective efforts and vision push us towards a brighter, more inclusive future.
