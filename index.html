At 13th Positive Foundation, we are dedicated to building a stronger, more vibrant community by inspiring hope, offering support, and instilling positive values in children and families. Through service, mentorship, and acts of goodwill, we empower individuals to overcome challenges and seize opportunities, fostering lasting change that uplifts Maywood and extends far beyond.

At 13th Positive Foundation, our mission is to uplift the community through service, mentorship, and compassion. Rooted in the heart of Maywood, we are dedicated to supporting children and families by providing essential resources, empowering opportunities, and fostering positivity. Through food and toy drives, scholarships, and educational programs, we strive to create lasting change that inspires hope and growth. With your support, we can extend our impact even further—building brighter futures, one act of kindness at a time. Together, we can nurture a stronger, more united community.
