Established in Brotherhood,
13th Positive aims to instill hope in the lives of children and their families through service, acts of goodwill, and mentorship.

At 13th Positive, we build a stronger community by fostering hope, support, and positive values in children and families. Through service, mentorship, and goodwill, we empower individuals to overcome challenges and embrace opportunities, creating lasting change that resonates throughout Maywood and beyond.
