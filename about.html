Who Are We

13th Positive Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to making a lasting difference in the lives of children. Founded in 2018, our mission is to empower individuals through education, mentorship, and community service.

Our dedicated members of 13th Positive Foundation are the driving force behind our mission. Each individual brings unique skills, experiences, and passion to help us make a positive impact on our community. From our founders to our newest team members, their collective efforts and vision push us towards a brighter, more inclusive future.

Instilling hope through brotherhood, goodwill, and mentorship.
